About me

My name is Anton, I'm trying to combine a hobby with making a living. In short I'd like to make programming for a living. I've got a technical Civil engineering background from a Linköping called "Applied physichs and electronics" in Linköping University from which I've completed 4/5 years. One thing I've found from my studies is that programming is more fun than most things and that one can create almost anything with it as a tool,
A job where I can utilize creativity with logic is hopefully what I'm after, programming is probably the red line here.
